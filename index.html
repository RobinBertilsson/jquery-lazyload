Lazy image loader Plugin based on jQuery

A really simple jQuery "addon" or "plugin" that allow's you to easily "lazy load" your heavy images with just one single line of javascript, thats pretty simple, right?

How does it work?

It's pretty straightforward... The only difference is that you need two images instead of one, basicly you'll load the tiny low resolution -image on pageload which will take about 10 miliseconds, then once the page is loaded the framework will take care of the rest.

How do i use it?

HTML
					<img class="js-lazy-load lazy-load" src="low-res.png" data-src="high-res.png">
Javascript
					$('.js-lazy-load').lazyLoad();
